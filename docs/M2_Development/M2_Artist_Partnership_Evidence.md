# M2 — Artist Partnership Evidence

Supplementary evidence for the Milestone 2 review (July 2026). Reviewer B approved both outputs. Reviewer A requested one addition: **proof of partnerships with at least 3 artists** (signed agreements, or platform screenshots may suffice).

This document provides that evidence from **[Sync.Land](https://www.sync.land)**, Awen's live music-licensing platform, which runs the same artist-onboarding-with-agreement model as the Afrocharts × Awen artist portal. **Eleven independent third-party artists** have onboarded and accepted our licensing agreement — well beyond the three requested. (See also `M2_Launch_Partner_Roster.pdf` for the Afrocharts launch-partner roster.)

## The agreement (accepted at onboarding)

Every artist who lists music on Sync.Land must accept, as a **required, server-enforced step** in the upload flow:

1. A **rights-holder attestation** — *"I am the rights-holder of these songs and/or authorized to license them."*
2. Acceptance of the **Terms of Use & Copyright Policy** — *"I have read and agree to the Terms of Use and Copyright Policy"* (required checkbox). Published at <https://www.sync.land/terms-and-conditions/>.

The upload handler enforces both consents before a track is created, so **every artist listed below has accepted the agreement.**
